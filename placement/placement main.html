Encouraging Entrepreneurship

  • AM IST Startup School
  • AM IST Startup School was created with a mission to facilitate entrepreneurial pursuits of AM IST sludents. It focuses on innovative applications for employment creation, wealth generation, social impact and global competition through creation of an entrepreneurial ecosystem.
  • This school is set to become one of the leading start-up incubators in the country through maximization of the success rate of young ventures from AM IST by empowering them with state-ol-the art technology, innovation capabilities as well as experiential business wisdom.
  • The programmes at AM IST Startup School follow a simple 4 step process in assisting students to take up Entrepreneurship as a career option.
  • Idea Generation Develop the Idea + Validate your Idea Mentoring & Funding

  • With success stories of King Travels, Fleet Moto, Repub Motors and many like them, AM IST Student Entrepreneurs are setting Benchmarks for others to follow.
