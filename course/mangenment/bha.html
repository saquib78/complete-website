Bachelor in Hospital Administration [BHA]



  • Bachelor in Hospital Administration (BHA) program is aimed at preparing healthcare professionals to future leaders who can effectively respond to the dynamic and ever changing healthcare industry. You will have regular contact with patients, community members, physicians, nurses, vendors, trustees, and organizational staff.



Eligibility for Bachelor in Hospital Administration [BHA]



  • 10+2 level of education in Science stream.
  • Biology as the main subject.
  • Minimum of 54 per cent marks at 10+2 level.
