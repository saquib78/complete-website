6 B Sc Biochemistry



This field is related to the study of chemicals and processes, that are directly related to living organisms. Just like the previous entry, this course also promises a good job in the research sector, if a student goes on to successfully complete B Sc and M Sc in the said field.




ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA



The candidate must pass in class 12/QE with minimum aggregate score of 55% in Science stream from a recognised school in India. Candidates awaiting results of HSC examination can apply too.

You must appear in amnest exam.

Seat are alloted on the basis of amnest.

8 B Sc Food Quality Assurance




Food is one of the basic necessities of mankind. With the rapid advancements in the field of technology, food manufacturing and processing also has seen big revolutions. This course is all about food processing, storage and quality assessment. Job opportunities are available in the form of private Food manufacturing companies, Government jobs etc.




ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA



Successful completion of the 10+2 level of education in the Science stream, completed from a recognized university. A minimum aggregate score of 55% (50% for SC/ST candidates) at the 10+2 level.

You must appear in amnest exam.

Seat are alloted on the basis of amnest.

9 B F Sc (Fisheries Science)



The fisheries industry is seeing a rise each year. The consumption is increasing each year. But slowly, the supply and demand gap is increasing. The aim of this field is to use scientific methods to improve the scenario and give fisheries sector a boost. Job prospects, like the previous entries, is good when it comes to this course too.




ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA



10+2 Science stream students with Physics, Chemistry and Biology subjects, who have passed 12th standard board examination from a recognized board are eligible to pursue this course. Also, to take part in the admission process, students must have appeared for state level entrance examination also.
