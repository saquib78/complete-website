3.B V Sc (Veterinary Science)



In common man’s terms, it is all about becoming animals’ Doctor! Looking from a scientific point of view, this course deals with training a person to diagnose and treat the diseases that affects animals.




ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA



10+2 Science (Biology Group) stream students, with Physics, Chemistry and Biology subjects, who have cleared 12th board examinations from a recognized Board are eligible to apply for this course.
Minimum 50% mark is required in the above mentioned subjects to be considered eligible to pursue this course. Relaxations in minimum marks are provided to SC/ST and OBC category students.
You must appear in amnest exam.

Seat are alloted on the basis of amnest.
