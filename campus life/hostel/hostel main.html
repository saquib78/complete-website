THE HOSTEL FACILITY




  1. The AM IST Hostel Facility comprises multistoreyed blocks. The hostel facility has been designed and constructed keeping in view the international standards and conducive ambiance for learning.

  2. The support staff provided at hostels is to facilitate your stay and make it as comfortable as possible. Please treat them with respect. If you have any problems with them, please direct your complaints to the Hostel Administration, rather than handling them yourself.

  3. You will have to act as custodians of the property; therefore, its usage should be done with care. You will be liable to pay for any loss or damage caused to it. In case of loss by a group of students, the residents of the entire floor/building will be fined. This is done with a view to encourage reporting of indisciplined students by the student themselves.

  4. AM IST treats all its students equally. Your roommate is entitled to equal rights of occupancy and access to any material of common use. We expect you to have a courteous attitude towards your roommate and not indulge in any activity that is not in accordance with common code of social conduct.

  5. The hostel provides a cot, a study table, a chair and a cupboard. Every hostel has solar hot water facility, mineral water, water cooler and common reading room. Gym and indoor games are available as common facility for the hostel students. Dining halls are conveniently located close to the hostels. A number of food courts are also available within the campus to cater to different food tastes.

  6. Free Wi-Fi facility (100 MB/per sec high speed and unlimited normal speed thereafter) is available to every student for purposes of academic pursuits.

  7. Laundry facility has been provided near the hostel which can be used by the inmates of the hostel.

  8. ATM and book shops are also located close to the hostels.

  9. Prayer halls for Hindus, Muslims and Christians are located adjacent to the hostel.







SANCTITY OF THE HOSTELS




  1. The hostels being a part of the University premises deserve a sacred status.

  2. Use of bad and abusive language should be avoided within the hostel / University premises.

  3. Students are not permitted to keep-in-their possession and in their rooms items such as crackers, dangerous weapons, firearms, contraband items or instruments that can cause serious injury to others.

  4. Students should not indulge in any illegal and/or unethical activity on the hostel premises.

  5. Smoking, consumption of alcoholic drinks and use of narcotic drugs in the hostel premises is strictly prohibited. Neither the students nor their visitors are allowed to bring or consume liquor, drugs, or any other intoxicants on the hostel premises. Severe action will be taken against those in possession of drugs or found under their influence.
