FURNISHINGS



  1. Standard Room Facilities:
    1. Single Cot

    2. Cupboard with self lock

    3. Individual study table and chair and electrical power point


  2. Electrical fittings will be provided by the hostel at the beginning of the occupancy, subsequently if there is any failure, it should be brought to the notice of the authorities immediately for replacement.

  3. Students are not supposed to take away common area fittings/furnishings to their individual rooms. Violators will be fined Rs 1,000/- per violation. Lift facility must be used as per permitted capacity. Any damage to lift will attract heavy fine

  4. Additional Requirements from Students:
    Demand of items of common use other than those mentioned above will be discussed in the Hostel Committee Meetings on a case-tocase basis. If the Committee feels that the item demanded are of common use, those items will be provided. Individual needs other than the ones mentioned above will be the responsibility of the individuals concerned, as long as they are not a cause of concern for other roommates and hostel residents.

  5. Furnishings and design of hostel buildings may vary as per design and structure of each individual building







SECURITY



  1. Whereas safety and security of furnishings for individual rooms will be the responsibility of the occupants of the particular room, for common areas or any other items provided for representatives will, in case of damage to the common area furnishings, provide names of the hostelites responsible for causing the damage

  2. To ensure security, it is requested that
    1. Guests are restricted to the lounge area only; and
    2. Each of the hostelites keeps his/her valuables under lock and key

  3. Valuables or cash other than the ones needed for immediate requirements will not be kept at the hostels. Small cash or items of necessity must be kept under lock and key.







ENTRY/EXIT TO/FROM HOSTELS



  1. For security reasons and to ensure that no unauthorized person enters the hostel premises, entry to hostels will strictly be restricted to the hostelites except for guests during specified hours and administration representatives. The students must keep their identity cards with them at all times.

  2. Hostelites will not be allowed to go out of the hostel after 9 pm or enter after 9.30 pm unless they have prior approval of the deputy warden. You must therefore plan in advance. Cases of the hostelites who fail to abide by security personnel instructions in this regard will be referred to the Disciplinary Committee. The Disciplinary Committee has full powers to take any action or punishment in the form of fine, suspension or expulsion from the hostel against the guilty.








VISITORS TO THE HOSTELS



  1. Male visitors to female hostels and female visitors to male hostels (other than parents) are NOT allowed.

  2. Parents may visit the hostels from 4.30 p.m. to 6.00 p.m. during working days and 8.30 a.m. to 6.00 p.m. during holidays.

  3. Use of hostels as a place of rest by the day-scholars is not permitted. Inmates from one hostel to another is not permitted without valid permission

  4. Overnight stay by visitors is not permitted. Overnight stay by parents will only be allowed under genuine circumstances. Advance approval of the Hostel Administration is necessary. Approved overnight stay will be charged at Rs 300/- per night

  5. All the visitors are required to sign the Visitorâ€™s book before entering the hostel.

  6. If it is established that an overnight stay by a visitor has taken place without the approval of the hostel administration, the host will be charged Rs 2,500/- per night per person as a penalty. In addition, such cases will be referred to the Disciplinary Committee for necessary action

  7. Visitors will only be restricted to the lounge(s)
