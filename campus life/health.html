  • A Safe and Secure Campus


  • For a safe academic ernvironment we have :

  • Dedicated team of professional security officers and personnel . under supervision of senior retired defence officers , deputed at various campus locations .
  • Network of 8500+ CCTV cameras , installed at strategic places, with a Control Room, providing 24 hour live surveillance / monitoring of nearly all places in the campus.
  • Automatic Boom Barriers are installed to regulate vehicular access in to the university.
  • Turnstile Entry at Main Gates, Libraries , Hostels / Residential areas to enhance security .
  • Fire Safety kit mounted inside all buildings and at all vulnerable and important locations.
  • University has its own Fire Tending vehicle with trained fire tending staff at the campus
